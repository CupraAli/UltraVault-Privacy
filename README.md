By using UltraVault you agree to the practices described below.

## 2. Summary

UltraVault is designed to be a privacy-respecting app:

- We do **not** use advertising.
- We do **not** use third-party analytics, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs.
- We do **not** collect your device identifier, advertising ID, location, contacts, or any other device data.
- Your disc collection can be kept **entirely on your device** — cloud sync is optional and off by default.

---

## 3. Information we collect

### 3.1 Account information

When you register, we collect and store on our servers:

- **Username** (3–30 characters)
- **Email address**
- **Password** — stored only as a secure one-way hash (we never see or store your plain-text password)
- An internal account identifier generated by our authentication system
- A flag indicating whether cloud sync is enabled for your account

### 3.2 Disc collection data

You enter data about the discs you own. This may include:

- Title, format (Blu-ray, 4K UHD, etc.), barcode, certificate, HDR/audio format, packaging, region, runtime, release date, purchase date
- Optional free-text fields: notes, owner label
- A poster image URL (retrieved from The Movie Database)
- Identifiers from The Movie Database (TMDb) and/or IMDb
- Storage location labels that you create yourself (e.g. "Living Room Shelf")
- An optional Letterboxd URL that you type in

This data is stored locally on your device. If you enable cloud sync, a copy is also stored on our servers so you can access it from other devices and share it with users you choose.

### 3.3 Sharing relationships

If you use the library sharing feature:

- A record is stored linking you to the user you invite (by email or username) and indicating whether the invitation is pending or accepted.
- Once accepted, the invited user gains **read-only** access to your disc collection.
- We do **not** read your device's contact list.

### 3.4 Authentication tokens

When you sign in, the app receives access and refresh tokens. These are stored securely on your device using the operating system's secure storage (Android Keystore). They are sent to our server with each request to authenticate you.

### 3.5 Information we do NOT collect

- No advertising identifier
- No device identifier (IMEI, MAC address, Android ID)
- No location data (the "Location" feature in the app refers to text labels for shelves, not geographic location)
- No contacts, phone number, SMS, calendar, microphone, or biometric data
- No analytics, usage telemetry, or crash reports
- No payment information (the app is free and contains no purchases at this time)

## 4. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| Internet | Communicate with our backend when cloud sync is enabled, and to look up movie information |
| Network state | Detect when the device is offline so sync can queue changes |
| Camera | **Only** used when you tap the barcode scan button, to read a product barcode. Images are not saved, uploaded, or transmitted |

---

## 5. Third-party services

UltraVault calls our own backend server (hosted on Microsoft Azure, UK region). Our backend, in turn, contacts the following third-party services on your behalf:

- **The Movie Database (TMDb)** — for film search results, posters and metadata. See [themoviedb.org/privacy-policy](https://www.themoviedb.org/privacy-policy).
- **OMDb API** — for supplementary film information such as certification. See [omdbapi.com](https://www.omdbapi.com/).

When you search for a film, your search query is forwarded to these services via our backend. No account information is sent to them.

We do **not** call Letterboxd or any other third party from the app.

---

## 6. How your data is used

We use the data described above only to:

- Create and authenticate your account
- Store and synchronise your disc collection across your devices when cloud sync is enabled
- Enable you to share your collection (read-only) with users you explicitly invite
- Look up film metadata when you request it

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

---

## 7. Data storage and security

- **On your device:** Collection data is stored in a local SQLite database. Authentication tokens are stored in the platform-secure store (Android Keystore).
- **On our servers:** If cloud sync is enabled, your data is stored in a Microsoft Azure SQL database in the UK region. All network traffic between the app and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS).
- **Passwords:** Stored only as salted one-way hashes using ASP.NET Core Identity's password hasher.

No system is 100% secure, but we use industry-standard encryption in transit and follow standard security practices.

## 8. Data retention and deletion

- If you uninstall the app, local data on the device is removed by the operating system.
- Disabling cloud sync stops further uploads, but previously uploaded data remains on our servers until you request deletion.
- To delete your account and all associated server-side data, email **[YOUR CONTACT EMAIL]** with the subject "Account Deletion". We will delete your account data within 30 days.
